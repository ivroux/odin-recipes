Chili

Description

This recipe is for an easy slow cooker chili.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 white onion
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 can hot red beans
  • 3 cans of diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup papika
  • 1/2 cup black pepper
  • 1/2 cup of onion powder
  • 1/2 cup of garlic powder

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef
  2. Sautee onion
  3. Drain kidney beans and diced tomatoes only
  4. Add everything to slow cooker and mix
  5. Cook for 8 hours
